What is cloud computing?

Cloud computing refers to any kind of hosted service delivered over the internet.
These services often include servers, databases, software, networks, analytics and other
computing functions that can be operated through the cloud.
Files and programs stored in the cloud can be accessed anywhere by users on the service,
eliminating the need to always be near physical hardware. In the past, for example, user-created
documents and spreadsheets had to be saved to a physical hard drive, USB drive or disk.
